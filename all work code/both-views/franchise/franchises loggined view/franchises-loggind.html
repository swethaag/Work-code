About Hakura
We are manufacturers of cold oil press, its accessories, water purifiers, water dispensers, and importers of spice mills, air fryers, deep fryers, and mopping robots. We have currently 3 outlets and we are planning to franchise 50 outlets in India. Each franchise outlet will hold INR 5-10 lakh of products and will sell that same value every month with a 20% contribution. Franchisee will buy min INR 5 lakh and there is no other deposit. We will provide training for sales and service.
